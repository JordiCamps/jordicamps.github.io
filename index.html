About Bookz

I am committed to protecting your privacy. This privacy policy explains how I handle any personal data I may obtain from you and how I ensure its protection.

No Data Collection

I am proud to state that Bookz is an entirely offline app that does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. My commitment to your privacy means I do not have any databases storing user data, nor do I engage in any data collection practices.

Offline Functionality

All functionalities of Bookz are available without any need for an internet connection. Data and preferences are stored locally on your device and are never sent to any server or third party.

Your Data, Your Device

Since Bookz operates offline, all data generated or used by the app remains on your device. I do not have access to this data, and it is your responsibility to manage and protect it.
